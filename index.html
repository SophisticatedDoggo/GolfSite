About Me

My name is Corey, and I’ve been working at Norvelt Golf Club as the Pro-Shop Manager since 2021. Golf has always been a huge part of my life, and I’ve spent years helping players enjoy the game and improve their performance.

I started regripping clubs after seeing how much of a difference fresh grips can make in a golfer’s confidence and consistency. What began as helping out fellow golfers has grown into something I take a lot of pride in—making sure every club feels right in your hands.

When you trust me with your clubs, you’re getting someone who genuinely cares about the game and the details that come with it. I treat every set as if it were my own and focus on delivering quality work you can rely on.

— Corey, Norvelt Golf Club
